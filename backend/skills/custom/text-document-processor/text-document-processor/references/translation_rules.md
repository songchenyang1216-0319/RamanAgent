# Translation Rules

## General principles

1. Preserve the original meaning.
2. Do not omit important information.
3. Use natural and fluent Chinese.
4. Keep technical terms accurate.
5. Keep names, dates, numbers, and formulas unchanged unless translation is required.

## Academic text

For academic documents:

- Keep terminology consistent.
- Preserve logical relationships.
- Translate long sentences into readable Chinese.
- Avoid overly casual language.

## Exam text

For exam documents:

- Preserve question numbers.
- Preserve options.
- Translate passages accurately.
- Do not reveal or change answers unless the user asks.
